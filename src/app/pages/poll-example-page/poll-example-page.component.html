Example & instructions
  • Listen recording by clicking on audio button
  • Decide its acoustic scene
  • Click and hold to drag audio buttons around
  • Drop them on gray fields above accoustic scene you selected
  • When you change listening recording the new one starts playing when the previous one stopped
  • In case you change your mind, you can swap elements or place them in the initial gray box
  • There is no audio replay limit
  • There is no time limit to make a choice
