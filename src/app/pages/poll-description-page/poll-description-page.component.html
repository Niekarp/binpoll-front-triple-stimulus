What will happen during the test?
  • In out test you will go through 10 similar matching tasks.
  • During each task you will be presented with an audio recording in three variations.
    • thus three audio recordings to listen to during each task
  • Those variations differ in acoustic scene they represent (described on the next page).
  • Your objective is to listen to the audio recordings variations and match them with the acoustic scenes you think they represent (an example of matching process is shown further in the test).
